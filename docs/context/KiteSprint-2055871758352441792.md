## Distilled design DNA

- **Photographic sticker as the atomic unit of the interface.** Subject-masked food photos behave like draggable, captionable, collectable objects — the primary "noun" of the app.
- **Dot-grid background** evokes a planner / bullet journal — quiet, structured, personal.
- **Two-color discipline:** off-white + near-black, with **sage green** as the single primary accent (CTAs, active tab pills) and **warm orange** reserved exclusively for kcal numerals. Photographs supply all other color.
- **Editorial typography:** large, confident headers; chunky bilingual pill labels (`细线 clean`, `虚线 rhythm`); handwritten captions overlaid on sticker art.
- **Chip-based selectors over sliders/steppers** — water amount, border style, duration.
- **Depth-of-field as navigation cue** — detail views blur adjacent items rather than hiding them, communicating "you're zoomed in on one of many."
- **AR/CV feels craft-like.** The cutout flow is framed (literally) as a creative ritual: place subject → ring outline → green check → kcal sticker → handwritten note.
- **Live Photo as output format.** The composed scrapbook saves to the camera roll as an animated Live Photo — bridging the app's diary aesthetic with the user's broader photo memory.
- **Iconography mood:** flat, slightly chunky, rounded — closer to Apple's SF Symbols than to commercial icon sets, but with custom illustrative pieces (the human water-figurine) anchoring hero moments.
